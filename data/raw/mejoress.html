Rise of Kingdoms Peerless Scholar Answers – Preliminary

Requirements: City Hall Level 10 and also the Lyceum of Wisdom Building

When? The preliminary questions can be answered from Monday 0:00 UTC to Friday 23:00 UTC

How?

  • You can participate once a day
  • The preliminary questions are 10 questions without time limit: You can search in google or of course check our answers guide
  • You can request help 3 times (per premliminary exam) to the alliance

Rewards:

  • 6 correct answers: qualify to that week Midterm
  • 9 correct answers: +1 Midterm Life Token (Max = 3 Midterm Life Tokens weekly)

Rise of Kingdoms Peerless Scholar Answers – Midterm

Requirements: 6 correct answers in any single session of preliminary

When? There are two sessions on Saturdays, at 02:30 UTC & at 12:30 UTC, but you can only participate in one of them. You can enter 5 minutes before the start, but if answering has already begun, you can’t enter.

How?

  • 15 multiple choice questions with timer:
    • 1st to 5th questions: 15 seconds per answer
    • 6th to 10th questions: 12 seconds per answer
    • 11th to 15th questions: 10 seconds per answer
  • If you answer incorrectly, or you don’t answer on time the midterm ends for you, but if you have Midterm life tokens you will be moved to the next question

Rewards:

  • 5 correct answers: some rewards
  • 10 correct answers: some rewards and also alliance reward
  • 15 correct answers: some rewards, alliance reward, portion of the gems pool and also qualify to the Final Exam & 3 Final Exam Life Tokens

Rise of Kingdoms Peerless Scholar Answers – Final

Requirements: 15 correct answers in any midterm exam

When? Last Saturday of the month at 14:30 UTC. You can enter 5 minutes before the start, but if answering has already begun, you can’t enter.

How?

  • 15 multiple choice questions with timer:
    • 1st to 5th questions: 15 seconds per answer
    • 6th to 10th questions: 12 seconds per answer
    • 11th to 15th questions: 10 seconds per answer
  • If you answer incorrectly, or you don’t answer on time the midterm ends for you, but you have 3 final exam life tokens so you can fail 3 questions

Rewards:

  • 5 correct answers: some rewards
  • 10 correct answers: some rewards and also alliance reward
  • 15 correct answers: some rewards, alliance reward, and also portion of the gems pool
